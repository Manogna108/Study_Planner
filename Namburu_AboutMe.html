Goals are a major factor in any person's life. They help you get a meaning of what you want to be in the future. My name is Manogna Namburu and I am a student at Dublin High School. My initial goal in life is to become a computer software engineer. Another main reason behind my profound interest is that both my parents are computer science engineers and so I hope that someday I would get the chance to follow in their footsteps. My plans to achieve this goal is to continue in the computer engineering academy at my high school so that I would get exposed to not only computer science but other fields related to engineering. Though my heart is set on computer science engineering, I might end up loving another field. Finally, once I am in college I would like to receive a degree in computer science engineering.
I am generally seen as a person who works hard to reach her goals and does not give up on the way because the workload is too much. Even at school projects I am seen to be self-motivated to get the work done on time. I personally believe that I would be a good computer engineer since I possess characteristics such as being alert at all times, organized and finally I am persistent, once I get engaged in some activity, I do not back out. In high school, some major accomplishments I have done so far are balancing my classes so that I get the work done on time and still do an admirable job on it. I am proud to have learned that I am able to handle any large amount of work given to me so far. Last year, I decided to take a bold decision and take computer science as my elective.
